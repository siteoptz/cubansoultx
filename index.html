About Cuban Soul

"Sabor Que Viene Del Alma"

At Cuban Soul, we believe that great food comes from the heart and soul. Our authentic Cuban recipes have been carefully crafted to bring you the true taste of Cuba, using traditional cooking methods and the finest ingredients.

Located in The Woodlands, TX, we serve the surrounding communities with fresh, made-to-order Cuban specialties from our mobile kitchen. Whether it's a family gathering, corporate event, or street festival, Cuban Soul brings the authentic flavors of the Caribbean to you.
